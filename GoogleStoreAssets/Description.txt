Download Bell plays a sound when a browser download completes or fails — 
so you can walk away from large downloads without constantly checking back.

FEATURES
- Custom sounds — upload any MP3, WAV, OGG, or AAC file for success and failure
- Per-file volume trim — balance loud/quiet files without touching master volume
- Master volume control
- Enable/disable toggle
- Works in Chrome, Brave, and Opera
- No data collected — all settings stored locally in your browser

SOUNDS THAT TRIGGER A NOTIFICATION
- Download completed → success sound
- Network or server error → failure sound
- User cancelled, browser closed, list cleared → silent

CUSTOM SOUNDS
Click the Upload button for either event to replace the built-in tone with your 
own audio file. A Level slider appears when a custom file is loaded, letting you 
trim that file's volume independently of the master.
